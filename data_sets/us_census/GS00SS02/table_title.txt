<p><b>Key Table Information:</b></p>

<p>See Appendix B in the state-level tables files at: https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/school-finances/data/tables.html for a description of state-specific reporting anomalies. State and national totals in this table are lower than the actual totals for these federal programs. This information includes only the revenue received by public school systems and excludes monies received by nonpublic school system organizations such as state agencies and private entities. The value of school lunch commodities is also excluded. Annual Survey of School System Finances statistics include the finances of charter schools whose charters are held directly by a government or a government agency. Charter schools whose charters are held by nongovernmental entities are deemed to be out of scope for the Annual Survey of School System Finances.</p>
